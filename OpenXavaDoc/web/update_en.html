OpenXava is a product that evolves continuously. In fact every day CVS is updated, and in weekly basis a stable version is tagged in CVS repository.
For apply patches and updates you only need to replace the code in OpenXava and OpenXavaTest projects from lastest code in the CVS. In Eclipse with Replace With > Lastest from HEAD over these projects.

In order to know the lastest changes, improvements and bug fixed in OpenXava you can subscribe to the mailing list openxava-changes@lists.sourceforge.net.
Or you may want to examine the resource history of OpenXava/history.txt resource.
