You are invited to participate actively in the forums, both for looking help or for discussing about OpenXava.

If you are looking for help, please, first look at the Reference Guide, after search on the previous posts in the forum, and then put your question in the help forums:
  • Help forum in English
  • Foro de ayuda en español
Don't hesitate to comment anything in your head about OpenXava. Do it in these forums:
  • Discussion forum in English
  • Foro de debate en español
Also there is a forum for communication within OpenXava team members, although it's public, therefore you can see the posts and comment if you want.
  • Developers forum
Moreover, OpenXava has a low traffic mailing list to notify new OpenXava CVS versions (with a list of improvements and fixes). If you work with CVS code maybe it's interesting that you subscribe to it:
  • openxava-changes mailing list
